Cooking Tips for Indian Recipes

Spice Blooming

Always bloom whole spices in hot oil first to release their essential oils and maximize flavor.

Onion Browning

Cook onions until golden brown for curries - this adds depth of flavor and natural sweetness.

Ginger-Garlic Paste

Use fresh ginger-garlic paste instead of powder for authentic taste in Indian dishes.

Yogurt in Curries

Add yogurt at room temperature and stir continuously to prevent curdling in creamy curries.

Rice Perfection

For perfect basmati rice, soak for 30 mins before cooking and use 1:1.5 rice to water ratio.

Tempering (Tadka)

Add tempering at the end for dal and curries to preserve the aromatic flavors of spices.

Marination Time

For tandoori dishes, marinate for at least 4 hours (overnight is best) for maximum flavor.

Roasting Spices

Dry roast whole spices before grinding to enhance their aroma and flavor in spice blends.
