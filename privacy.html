Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2023

Welcome to Flavor Fexo Food ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the Site.

By using our Site, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and we will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Site, including:

Personal Identification Information

  • Name: We collect your name when you register for an account, subscribe to our newsletter, or participate in surveys or contests. This helps us personalize your experience and address you properly in communications.
  • Email Address: Your email is collected for account creation, newsletter subscriptions, and communication purposes. We use it to send order confirmations, updates, and marketing materials if you've opted in.
  • Mailing Address: For delivery of physical products or gifts, we collect shipping addresses. This information is only used for order fulfillment and not shared beyond necessary shipping partners.
  • Phone Number: We may collect phone numbers for account verification, order updates, or customer service purposes. This helps us provide better support and faster resolution to issues.
  • Payment Information: When making purchases, we collect payment details like credit card numbers through secure payment processors. We don't store full payment details on our servers for security reasons.

Non-Personal Identification Information

  • Browser Information: We collect data about the browser type and version you're using to optimize our site's performance and ensure compatibility across different platforms.
  • IP Address: Your IP address helps us understand your approximate location for regional content customization and detect potential fraudulent activities.
  • Device Type: Information about your device (mobile, tablet, desktop) allows us to optimize the display and functionality of our Site for your specific device.
  • Operating System: We collect OS data to troubleshoot technical issues and improve user experience across different platforms.
  • Usage Data: This includes information about how you interact with our Site - pages visited, time spent, links clicked - which helps us improve content and navigation.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide and Maintain Our Service: Your information helps us operate and maintain our website, ensuring all features function properly and efficiently for all users.
  • Improve User Experience: By analyzing how users interact with our Site, we can make improvements to navigation, content layout, and feature implementation.
  • Process Orders and Transactions: We use your personal and payment information to complete purchases, process payments, and deliver ordered products or services.
  • Customer Support: Your information allows us to respond more effectively to service requests, troubleshoot issues, and provide personalized assistance.
  • Marketing and Promotions: With your consent, we may use your information to send newsletters, special offers, and updates about new recipes or services.
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: We analyze data to detect and prevent fraudulent activities, unauthorized access, and other security risks.
  • Legal Compliance: Your information may be used to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes, such as responding to subpoenas.
  • Personalization: We use collected data to tailor content, recommendations, and advertisements to your interests and preferences.
  • Analytics and Research: Aggregated data helps us understand user behavior, preferences, and trends to improve our services and develop new features.
  • Account Management: Your information enables us to manage your account, verify your identity, and provide access to account-specific features.

We may share your personal information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may employ third-party companies to facilitate our services, such as payment processors or shipping companies. These providers have access only to the information needed to perform their functions.

  • Advertising Partners: We may share limited information with third-party advertising partners to deliver targeted advertisements, but not personal identifiers without consent.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Site and hold certain information to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly, enabling basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: These collect information about how visitors use our Site to help us improve its performance and user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies: These allow the website to remember choices you make (like language preferences) to provide enhanced, more personal features.
  • Targeting Cookies: These are used to deliver ads more relevant to you and your interests, and to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use tools like Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our Site, which helps us improve content and functionality.
  • Social Media Cookies: These are set by social media services we've added to our Site to enable you to share content with your networks.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Site. For more information about cookies and how to manage them, visit our Cookie Policy page.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: We use SSL/TLS encryption to protect data transmissions between your browser and our servers, ensuring sensitive information remains secure.
  • Secure Storage: Personal information is stored on secure servers with limited access, protected by firewalls and other security technologies.
  • Access Controls: We implement strict access controls, ensuring only authorized personnel with a legitimate need can access your personal data.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security assessments and audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities in our systems.
  • Employee Training: Our staff receives regular training on data protection best practices and privacy policies to ensure proper handling of your information.
  • Incident Response: We have procedures in place to detect, report, and investigate any personal data breaches in accordance with legal requirements.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect and retain the minimum amount of personal data necessary for our legitimate business purposes.
  • Secure Payments: All payment transactions are processed using PCI-DSS compliant services, and we never store full credit card details on our servers.

  • Right to Access: You can request copies of your personal data that we hold, including what information we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances, such as when it's no longer necessary for the purposes collected.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may request that we limit how we use your personal data in certain situations, such as during a dispute about data accuracy.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request transfer of your data to another organization or directly to you in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Object: You may object to our processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes or where we rely on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent to process data, you can withdraw that consent at any time without affecting prior processing.
  • Right to Complain: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe we've violated data protection laws.
